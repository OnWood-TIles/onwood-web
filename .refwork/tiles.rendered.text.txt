Why OnWood
We don't sell tiles. We finish rooms.
Every OnWood project starts on-site, in your light, at your subfloor. We measure twice, dry-lay the pattern, and hand-set every board and tile so the grain runs the way the room wants it to.
Coolum to Caloundra, we've spent years learning what humidity, salt air and Queensland sun do to a floor — so yours lasts.
